Nordic Knots

Tailored for a discerning audience, this website exudes tranquility and sophistication through a harmonious blend of visually soothing pastel hues. Each element is thoughtfully curated to evoke a sense of calm and refinement, offering visitors an emotionally engaging journey through elegant aesthetics.

Visit website

Design Process: I find inspiration on Dribbble and Land-book to refine my vision and stay updated on design trends, ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing outcome.This process helps me envision the desired look and feel before translating it into the final design. The logo and favicons were crafted by me using Photoshop.

Challenges: Ensuring consistent design across various screen sizes and managing multiple HTML documents while maintaining design's harmony.

Technologies Used: Implemented BEM methodology in HTML structure, and enhanced stylesheet organization by leveraging Sass. Additionally, utilized Flexbox and CSS Grid to create fluid and responsive layouts for optimal user experience.

Responsive Design: Used responsive design principles, I ensured the project's adaptability across devices and screen sizes. This comprehensive approach guarantees a seamless and visually pleasing experience for all users, regardless of the device they're using.

User Experience (UX): I crafted intuitive navigation, clear layout, and seamless interactions to ensure effortless browsing and engagement.

Features: a login popup form, product cards displaying pricing and availability, a discount coupon redemption feature, subtle on-hover animations for enhanced interactivity, and showcasing custom logo and favicon designs this website showcases my skills in user-friendly design, intuitive functionality, and creative branding as well as search engine optimization.
