My website serves as a culmination of my journey in web design, showcasing the skills and knowledge I've acquired along the way. Drawing from my experiences and learnings, I've meticulously crafted every aspect of the website, from its layout and typography to its functionality and user experience. Furthermore, I've leveraged my understanding of design principles and color theory to create a cohesive and engaging visual identity.

Visit website

Design Process: In the design process, I found inspiration in the work of fellow web designers, ultimately opting for a dark palette to achieve a modern and elegant appearance. This choice not only lends a sleek and sophisticated aesthetic to the website but also enhances visual contrast, ensuring that key elements stand out effectively. I aimed to create a captivating and memorable user experience that resonates with visitors and communicates a sense of professionalism and sophistication.

Challenges: I faced challenges in maintaining design consistency across pages, selecting suitable components and animations, and organizing files efficiently. One highlight was creating a small interactive effect with JavaScript: when hovering over one of the three category elements, it expands and subtly pushes the others aside. Coming up with this idea during the design process helped me explore how motion and interactivity can enhance the overall user experience while keeping the interface balanced and engaging.

Technologies Used: This project features HTML, CSS, SASS and JavaScript for dynamic user experiences. It's responsive across devices and utilizes Git for efficient code management. These technologies demonstrate my proficiency in modern web development."

Responsive Design: Designing my website with a focus on responsiveness and accessibility maintains a modern appearance while ensuring usability for all. Its responsive design seamlessly adapts to different devices, while adherence to accessibility standards ensures inclusivity for all users.

Interaction & Usability: In designing my website, I prioritized intuitive navigation, clear layouts, and engaging aesthetics. By leveraging modern design trends and focusing on user-centricity, I aimed to enhance overall satisfaction and usability. Through a carefully mapped experience, visitors are guided seamlessly from initial navigation to conversion points, ensuring a memorable and impactful journey.

Features: The website offers a comprehensive experience, featuring dedicated project pages, contact details, categorized projects, and a succinct landing page introduction. With responsive design and intuitive navigation, visitors can explore my work effortlessly, while engaging visuals enhance the presentation.
