zen studio

Dynamic website crafted in a single HTML document. The landing page warmly welcomes visitors with a tranquil and inviting design, setting the tone for the entire website experience. Clean and uncluttered design, allowing visitors to easily find the information they need without distractions.

Visit website

Design Process: Focused on a meticulous design process that prioritized simplicity and impact, the website was carefully designed using selection of a color scheme that resonates with the target audience. Opting for a simple yet impactful palette, I aimed to create a visually cohesive and engaging experience for visitors. The logo and favicons were crafted by me using Photoshop.

Challenges: Overcoming technical challenges such as implementing the navigation menu for mobile and smaller ddevices, optimizing performance, troubleshooting compatibility issues across different browsers. It was challening to manage and integrate diverse content elements, including text, images and interactive features, while maintaining consistency and coherence throughout the website.

Technologies Used: Adopted the BEM methodology alongside Sass to maintain a clear and organized structure within the HTML and CSS. Employing Flexbox and CSS Grid, I crafted fluid layouts that adapt seamlessly to various screen sizes, ensuring an optimal user experience across devices. Additionally, I incorporated basic JavaScript functionalities to enhance interactivity, particularly focusing on refining the menu for mobile and smaller screen users.

Responsive Design: I implemented a responsive design approach, starting with desktop layouts and utilizing media queries to adapt seamlessly to smaller screens. This ensured a consistent user experience across all devices, prioritizing desktop users while maintaining usability for mobile and tablet users.

User Experience (UX): Applied UI/UX design principles throughout the project, focusing on intuitive navigation, clear layout, and seamless interactions to enhance user experience: the layout was carefully structured to prioritize content visibility and readability, the optimized the usage of whitespace to enhance readability and focus

Features: A captivating landing page, drawing visitors in with its visual charm, an immersive "about" section to grasp the school's ethos and goals, a section about teachers, fostering personal connections with instructors. Additionally the "classes" section offers diverse options with detailed insights for informed choices and also a user-friendly sign-up form.
